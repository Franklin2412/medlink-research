Executive Summary

Healthcare has transitioned from experimental AI to operational clinical integration. This repository serves as a professional guide for clinicians navigating the implementation of Large Language Models (LLMs) in modern medical workflows.

The primary focus of current industry adoption is toward reducing cognitive load and administrative friction while enhancing diagnostic precision through multimodal data analysis.
